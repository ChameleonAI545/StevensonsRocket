A BRIEF SUMMARY

Africa Rocket is building a maternal and newborn healthcare system that continuously learns and evolves toward a clear goal: high-quality care for every mother and baby at a price the world can afford.

The goal defines the system. Rather than implementing fixed solutions, we build a learning system that improves over time—becoming ever more effective, ever more efficient, and ever more impactful.

Most systems optimise processes. We are building a system that optimises itself.
